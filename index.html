Lovebirds and Bird lovers

One day you suddenly catch me from the sky.
You are holding me in your big warm hands.
I must be a beautiful bird, as you are observing and caressing me with so much love and care.
As time passes you realize you've seen all my colors, and you conclude I look like other birds, I am maybe not the special bird you were looking for.
Then suddenly you toss me up in the air.
As I fly away, confused and unsteady but also quite relieved, you walk away without looking back.
